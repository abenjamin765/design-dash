# /orca-research-plan

Loads `skills/2-research/ux-research-planner/SKILL.md`.

Plans moderated and unmoderated UX research studies aligned to ORCA phases — interviews, usability tests, card sorts, tree tests, surveys, and more.

## Usage

```
/orca-research-plan
```

## What it does

1. Loads `skills/2-research/ux-research-planner/SKILL.md`
2. Interviews you about the ORCA phase, research questions, and constraints
3. Recommends method, participant criteria, and sample size with rationale
4. Generates a complete research plan: objectives, screener, discussion guide or task list, analysis plan

Outputs to `dashes/{slug}/research/YYYY-MM-DD-{slug}-plan.md`.

## Related commands

- `/orca-synthesize` — synthesize findings after the study runs
- `/design-dash` — full orchestrated dash (includes research at P1)
