Questions we expect

Is Green Loom a POS system?

Not initially. Green Loom is a modular layer around the tools regulated retailers already use.

Is Green Loom only for cannabis?

Cannabis is the starting point; the vision is high-regulation retail.

Will it replace our current tools?

Not necessarily — it connects and extends existing systems first.

Will it work with WordPress?

Yes — WP themes and plugins are core to the plan. This site is WordPress.

What stage is Green Loom in?

Early development — module statuses above are kept honest.

How can we get involved?

Join the list or schedule a call — especially teams with complex menus, delivery, or compliance burdens.
